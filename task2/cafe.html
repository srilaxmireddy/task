PANTRY CAFE

Pantry Café is a Dubai-based gourmet café and delicatessen that brings you quality and flavorful comfort food from around the world, using only the finest and freshest ingredients – all of which are cooked with love, care and passion from our open kitchen

Our menu features a scrumptious array of choices ranging from tapas, starters, gourmet burgers, steaks and grills to sandwiches and pizzas. We pride ourselves on producing wholesome-good-for-you food, and do our best to support local farms by using locally produced organic fruits and vegetables wherever possible.

Established in 2012 in Al Wasl Square, Pantry Café has recently expanded and opened its second Dubai branch in Bay Square in Business Bay.
